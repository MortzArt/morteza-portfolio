About .ME
I'm Morteza. A designer by craft, and an engineer by training, but a problem solver by nature.

With 7+ years in product design and over a decade of total experience in graphic design and marketing, I work at the intersection of business complexity and human simplicity. My engineering background provides a systematic lens. I don't just design interfaces; I design outcomes.

I handle the end-to-end process, taking products from high-level strategy through to scalable design systems and high-fidelity execution. With my deep roots in brand and marketing, I ensure every product doesn't just function perfectly; it tells a story worth acting on.

Based in Barcelona and working with global teams, I care deeply about mentorship, scalable systems, and the constant pursuit of learning. Whether it's a new technology or a deeper understanding of human behavior, I believe staying curious is the only way to make technology feel human again.
